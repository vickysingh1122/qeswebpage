Site Walk for DFS Consultancy

The Design for Safety Regulation requires a Design Safety and Fire Professional (DSFP) to be appointed by the developer for construction projects of over $10 million. The purpose of this requirement is to identify and mitigate design risks through collective protective measures. The DSFP conducts site walks with designers and consultants to understand the designs and provide advice to the developer on risk reduction. Safety review meetings are held after each walkthrough to assess progress towards eliminating or reducing risks.

Fabrication work – Catwalk and Ladder

The importance of workplace safety cannot be overstated, and QES offers customised solutions to enhance safety and accessibility in various industrial and commercial settings. Two essential safety provisions provided by QES are catwalks and cage ladders. Catwalks are elevated walkways that enable safe access to hard-to-reach areas such as rooftops, equipment platforms, or machinery installations. Cage ladders are vertical access structures equipped with safety cages surrounding the climbing rungs or steps. These structures are designed to provide secure access to elevated areas, ensuring the well-being of personnel and facilitating maintenance operations. The engineers at QES specialise in designing and fabricating safety provision services to significantly improve the safety of their clients’ workplaces.

Safety Reviews and Promotion

Safety promotion plays an important role in a company’s occupational health and safety management system. It is a crucial aspect in ensuring a secure and healthy work environment for employees. Singapore has implemented robust initiatives to promote workplace safety across industries, with a strong emphasis on occupational safety and health. We have assisted over 40 companies/projects in Singapore by designing, planning, and coordinating safety promotion activities to encourage and foster workplace safety and health. Our experienced consultant has been working in this field for many years and has successfully handled projects involving various stakeholders.

Fire Risk Compliance and WSH Consultancy Services

In the telecommunications sector, QESafety provides specialised Fire Risk Compliance and Workplace Safety and Health (WSH) Consultancy Services, ensuring industry-specific safety requirements are met. With a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced in this sector, our experienced team offers comprehensive support in achieving fire risk compliance and maintaining a safe working environment. Our services include conducting thorough fire risk assessments, developing customised fire safety plans, implementing preventive measures, and providing WSH training to employees. With our expertise and extensive experience in the telecommunications sector, we assist organisations in effectively managing fire risks, promoting workplace safety, and fostering a culture of safety within their operations.
